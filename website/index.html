About

Hello there, my name is Nyleve Alejandrino. I am a multi-disciplinary designer with a focus on Web and Graphic who takes pleasure in planning, developing and creating goal driven projects and watching them come to life and deliver results.

Graphic Design

While I enjoy making things look more aesthetically pleasing as the next guy, I focus on ‘goal oriented’ design. These past years I’ve been honing this mindset with every passing project spanning from Branding, Flyers to App Mockups.

Web Design

With the rise of ‘Responsive design’ and a focus on ‘User interface’ and ‘User Experience’, Web Design has become a more demanding discipline. I believe in developing simple yet elegant web experiences that maximize a ‘call to action’.

Motion Graphics

From UI/UX, Banners, Kinetic Typography to App mockups I take pixel perfect care in tailoring motion graphics to engage an audience to create the perfect pitch that gets your message across.
